About CodeFit_AI.js

CodeFit_AI.js is ${DarkMode} Devs' online AI bootcamp — a web app built around a simple idea: help people find out whether coding fits them before they spend years and money finding out the hard way.

New learners start with a short fit assessment — an original problem-solving quiz plus a public-domain Big Five work-style questionnaire — that gives an encouraging read on their strengths and suggests tech roles that tend to suit them. Finishing it unlocks the lessons: a beginner curriculum in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, taught by an AI tutor you can switch between roles — code explainer, debugger, optimization expert, or curriculum guide. The tutor sees the topic you're on and the code in your sandbox, so its help targets what you're actually working on.

The tutor sits alongside an in-browser sandbox — separate HTML, CSS, and JavaScript editors with a live preview and a Run button — and the curriculum tracks your progress as you complete topics. You can learn by typing or turn on voice mode to speak your questions and hear the replies read back. It works on phones too, where it becomes a chat-only tutor, and your progress, saved snippets, and preferences follow you across devices.

The app runs entirely on Firebase and is in active development by the ${DarkMode} Devs team — new curriculum topics and features ship continuously.
